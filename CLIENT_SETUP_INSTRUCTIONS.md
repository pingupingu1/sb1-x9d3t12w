## Welcome to Vitallic!

Your Dutch AI Voice Assistant is ready to use. Follow these simple steps to get started.

### What You Need
- **Browser**: Google Chrome or Microsoft Edge (recommended)
- **Microphone**: Must be working and not muted
- **Internet**: Stable connection required
- **Account**: Email and password (you'll create one on first access)

---

## Step 1: Access the Application

Your unique application URL is:
```
https://[your-deployment-url]
```

**Save this bookmark for future use!**

---

## Step 2: Create Your Account (First Time Only)

1. Open the URL in Chrome or Edge
2. Click "Don't have an account? Sign up"
3. Enter your email and password
4. Click "Create Account"
5. Check your email and confirm your account
6. Return to the app and log in with your credentials

---

## Step 3: Grant Permissions

When you click the microphone button for the first time:
1. Browser will ask for microphone access
2. Click "Allow"
3. This only needs to be done once

**Important**: If you accidentally blocked microphone access:
- Chrome: Click the camera icon in the address bar and change permissions
- Edge: Click the info icon in the address bar and change permissions

---

## Step 4: Start Using Vitallic

### The Assistant Tab
1. **Select a Voice Profile**:
   - "Friendly & Casual" - For casual conversations
   - "Professional & Business-Friendly" - For business calls

2. **Click the Blue Microphone Button**
   - The microphone will turn red while listening
   - You'll see "Luisteren..." (Listening...)

3. **Speak in Dutch**
   - Speak clearly at normal volume
   - Keep sentences concise
   - Example: "Hallo Vitallic"

4. **Listen to Response**
   - Vitallic will respond in Dutch
   - Wait for the response to finish

5. **Continue Conversation**
   - Click microphone again to speak
   - Repeat step 3-4

6. **End Conversation**
   - Click the red microphone button to stop

### The Dashboard Tab
1. **View Statistics**
   - Total Conversations: All calls made
   - Active Conversations: Currently running
   - Total Duration: Time spent in conversations
   - Average Duration: Average per call

2. **Call History**
   - See all your conversations
   - Click any call to view full transcript
   - Check status: Active, Completed, or Failed

3. **Transcript Viewer**
   - Read exact words from conversations
   - See timestamps for each message
   - Check confidence scores

### Speech Recognition
- Speak clearly and at a normal pace
- Minimize background noise
- Use your microphone's normal volume
- Take pauses between sentences
- Speak in Dutch for best results

### Voice Quality
- The voice quality depends on your system
- Different computers may sound different
- All voices are in Dutch

### Browser Settings
- Keep browser up to date
- Disable ad blockers if having issues
- Use latest version of Chrome or Edge

## Troubleshooting

### Issue: Microphone Button Doesn't Work
**Solution**:
1. Refresh the page
2. Check microphone is not muted
3. Check browser permissions (allow microphone)
4. Try different browser if issue persists

### Issue: No Sound from Vitallic
**Solution**:
1. Check computer volume is not muted
2. Check browser tab volume (check browser volume icon)
3. Try different browser
4. Restart the app

### Issue: Speech Not Recognized
**Solution**:
1. Speak more clearly
2. Reduce background noise
3. Check microphone is working in system settings
4. Try speaking louder
5. Ensure internet connection is stable

### Issue: App is Slow or Laggy
**Solution**:
1. Close other browser tabs
2. Close other applications
3. Refresh the page
4. Check your internet speed
5. Try different browser

### Issue: Dashboard Not Loading
**Solution**:
1. Refresh the page
2. Check internet connection
3. Clear browser cache
4. Try private/incognito mode
5. Contact support
